1. Reason for Visit 
•	New patient / second opinion
•	Chemotherapy or immunotherapy visit
•	Toxicity / side effect management
•	Surveillance / remission follow-up
•	Lab review or imaging review
•	Symptom check / urgent visit
•	Telehealth / refill

2. What We Found 
•	Summary of most recent imaging, biopsy, pathology, or genetic testing
•	Key lab results (CBC, CMP, tumor markers)
•	Performance status / weight / vital trends
•	Any new findings or progression vs stable disease

3. Treatment Summary
•	Current oncologic medications or regimens (name, dose, schedule)
•	Any recent changes (hold, dose reduction, delay, switch)
•	Supportive medications (antiemetics, growth factors, steroids, anticoagulants)
•	Drug interactions or precautions reviewed

4. What We Discussed / Decided
•	Goals of treatment: curative, palliative, maintenance, surveillance
•	Response assessment: how the cancer is responding
•	Side effects: which ones to watch for, how to manage them
•	Symptom management / palliative care discussion if relevant
•	Clinical trial options if applicable

5. Plan Going Forward 
•	Medication plan: start / continue / change / stop
•	Imaging and labs: which, when, and purpose
•	Referrals: nutrition, genetics, palliative care, radiation, surgery, PT, social work, fertility, etc.
•	Next visit/follow up: when and what will be reviewed

6. Administrative / Practical Notes
•	Medication refills
•	Advance directives or code status (if relevant)
