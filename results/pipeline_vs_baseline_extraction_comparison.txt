BREAST SECTION NOTE — Genetic_Testing_Results Pathology Contamination:
Many breast samples (ROW 1, 5, 6, 11, 12, 13, 16) have IHC pathology
(ER/PR/HER2 status) incorrectly included in Genetic_Testing_Results.
The prompt excludes these, but the model still includes them. This is
a known issue that can be fixed with a post-hook to strip IHC data.
The correctly captured genetic results (BRCA1 in ROW 4, MSH2 in ROW 7,
Mammaprint in ROW 8, Myriad in ROW 14, BRCA in ROW 17, ATM in ROW 18)
demonstrate the field works for its intended purpose.
